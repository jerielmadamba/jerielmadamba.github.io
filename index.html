About Me

As a Data Analyst/Data Scientist, I am passionate about utilizing data to tackle complex business challenges. With over 8 years of combined experience in HR analytics, manufacturing, and supply chain, I have developed a skill set in identifying areas for optimization and efficiency improvements that help organizations meet their objectives.

My proficiency in various technical tools and programming languages, such as Python, Machine Learning, R, SQL, SAS, MS Excel, Looker, Tableau, and PowerBI, enables me to effectively analyze and interpret complex data sets, recognize patterns and trends, and provide practical insights that guide companies towards data-driven decisions.

I have a background in industrial engineering and have made a shift to data analytics. I firmly believe that there is no such thing as "best", as there is always a better way to do things.
